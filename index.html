What is Drive Gallery Pro?

Sync Your Media,
Simplify Your Workflow

Drive Gallery Pro is the bridge between your Google Drive and your WordPress site. We designed it for users who want to host high-resolution galleries without worrying about server storage or slow loading times.

By leveraging the power of Google's global infrastructure, your images and videos are served instantly to your visitors, while you maintain full control over your media directly from your Drive folders.

Setup Documentation

Three Steps to Launch

1

Connect API

Enable Google Drive API in your Cloud Console and enter your Client ID & Secret in the plugin settings.

2

Authorize

Click "Connect Google Account" to authorize read-only access. No complicated file permissions needed.

3

Display

Use the Elementor widget or the powerful shortcode to showcase your media collection anywhere.

[drvgp_gallery id="YOUR_FOLDER_ID"]

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Drive Gallery Pro really free?
⊕
Yes! The plugin is 100% free and open-source under the GPLv2 license. You can use it on personal and commercial projects without any hidden fees.
Will it slow down my website?
⊕
Absolutely not. Unlike traditional gallery plugins that store images on your server, we serve media directly from Google's high-speed global infrastructure. Combined with our AJAX navigation, your site remains lightning-fast.
Do I need to be a developer to set it up?
⊕
No technical skills required! Just follow our 3-step setup guide. If you can copy and paste a Client ID, you can use Drive Gallery Pro.
Is it compatible with Elementor?
⊕
Yes, we have native Elementor support. A dedicated widget will appear in your Elementor sidebar once the plugin is activated, allowing for full drag-and-drop customization.
Can I display videos from Google Drive?
⊕
Yes! The plugin supports both images and video files. Our premium lightbox is optimized to play Google Drive videos smoothly on all devices.
